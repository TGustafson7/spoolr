Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Spoolr does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

What Data Spoolr Stores

Spoolr stores your concert history — artists, venues, dates, companions, moods, notes, and photos — entirely on your device using local storage. None of this data ever leaves your phone.

What Data We Collect

None. Spoolr has no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no user accounts. We don't know who you are, what shows you've been to, or how you use the app.

Third-Party Services

Spoolr does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs collecting data in the background.

Sharing

When you share a show with a friend, Spoolr uses the standard iOS share sheet to send a message. The shared content goes directly from your device to your friend — it never passes through our servers because we don't have any.

Photos

If you attach photos to a show, they are stored locally on your device within the app's data. Spoolr does not upload, sync, or transmit your photos anywhere.

Children's Privacy

Spoolr does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how Spoolr handles data, we'll update this page. But honestly, the plan is simple: your data is yours, and it stays on your device.

Contact

Questions? You can reach us through the Spoolr support page on the App Store or by leaving feedback in the app.
